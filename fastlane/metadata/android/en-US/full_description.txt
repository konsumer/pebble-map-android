Pebble Maps Nav mirrors Google Maps turn-by-turn navigation onto your Pebble watch. Start
driving directions in Google Maps on your phone and the next maneuver — arrow, distance,
street, and a trip summary (time left, distance left, ETA) — appears on the watch
automatically, on a single glanceable screen. The watchapp even pops up on its own when
navigation begins.

How it works: Google has no public live-navigation API, so this app reads the ongoing
Google Maps navigation notification (via the notification listener) and forwards each update
to the watch over PebbleKit Android 2. The maneuver arrow is Google's own icon, so it stays
correct across languages and maneuver types.

Requirements:
* A Pebble watch with the companion "Maps Nav" watchapp installed (from the Rebble appstore).
* The Pebble mobile app, paired with your watch.
* Notification access granted to this app (so it can read the Maps navigation notification).

Notes:
* Android only. iOS sandboxes notifications, so this approach is not possible there.
* This app does not collect or transmit any personal data. It only reads the Google Maps
  navigation notification and sends the directions to your watch.
* Fully open source — see the source repository for the exact, CI-built releases.
